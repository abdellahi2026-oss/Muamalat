# **App Name**: Muamalat Manager

## Core Features:

- Murabaha Contract Management: Create, edit, view, and archive Murabaha (بيع مؤجّل) contracts with specific fields for client, goods, units, price, payment method, and dates.
- Mudarabah Contract Management: Create, edit, view, and archive Mudarabah (مضاربة) contracts including profit sharing ratios, capital details, and investment area.
- Musharakah Contract Management: Create, edit, view, and archive Musharakah (مشاركة) contracts, managing partner contributions, ownership percentages, and profit/loss distribution.
- Wakalah Contract Management: Create, edit, view, and archive Wakalah (وكالة بأجر) contracts with details on the client, agent, agency type, duration, and agent's fee structure.
- Commodity Card Management: Manage commodity cards, tracking card numbers, nominal values, issuing bodies, and statuses. This 'tool' helps determine appropriate use across Murabaha, Mudarabah, and Musharakah contracts.
- Dashboard Overview: A dashboard displaying the number of active contracts, total amounts, expected profits, and alerts for upcoming or overdue contracts.
- Transaction Filtering and Alerts: Filter contracts by type, payment status, client, or counterparty.  Set up notifications for contracts nearing due dates or with overdue payments.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#031185) to create a calm and trustworthy environment, evoking feelings of stability and tradition.
- Background color: White (#FFFFFF) to maintain a light and airy feel that does not distract from content, complementing the soft blue.
- Accent color: Light grey (#D3D3D3) as highlights for interactive elements and important information to reflect the value and significance of financial transactions.
- Body and headline font: 'Cairo', sans-serif, provides readability and a modern feel appropriate for financial documents and contracts.
- Use simple, clear icons to represent different contract types and actions, ensuring ease of use in the RTL interface.
- Employ a card-based layout with a mobile-first approach, presenting information in concise summaries with RTL support.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback on user interactions without being distracting, ensuring smooth navigation in the RTL interface.
